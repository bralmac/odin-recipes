Caesar Salad recipe

Recipe for Caesar Salad:

This recipe will make a large salad for two very hungry people.

You will need the following ingredients to prepare the chicken:

  • 8 raw chicken tenders
  • 4 heads of little gem lettuce (2 heads per person)
  • 20g olive oil (for cooking tenders)
  • 50g olive oil
  • 150g panko breadcrumbs (makes more than you need for salad)
  • 10 ounces ground pork
  • 20g chopped capers
  • ▪Grated parmesan

Cooking the chicken:

  1. Preheat medium sauté pan over medium heat. Add squeeze of olive oil, and place tenders in pan. It's ok if they're cozy in the pan,w e're searing and steaming.
  2. Cover and allow to cook for about 5 minutes. Flip tenders, cover again, and reduce heat to low to cook for another 2-3 minutes. Tenders are done when internal temp reaches 145F/62C. Remove from pan and allow to rest briefly.
  3. Wipe oil out of pan, increase heat to med-high, and add 50g. Once shimmering, add breadcrumbs. Toss to combine and add pinch of salt.
  4. Continue stirring and tossing while cooking to a nice golden brown for about 60 seconds. Remove from pan and allow to dry and cool on a towel.

You will need the following ingredients for the dressing:

  • 1 large egg
  • 45g grated parmesan
  • 30g or 1 can of oil packed anchovies, rinsed well (SUBSTITUTE 25g Worcestershire if you don't like anchovies + add 15g more parmesan)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 50g lemon juice
  • 10g water
  • 275g neutral oil (canola, light olive oil, avocado, etc)

Preparing the dressing and assembling the salad:

  1. Add all ingredients except oil to tall sided container and blend with immersion blender until solids are broken down then slowly stream in oil while blending.
  2. Add lettuce, chopped capers, 4 very large pinches of grated parm, 4 very large pinches of breadcrumbs, chicken tenders (chopped), and top with 4-6+ spoons of caesar to your taste. Top with additional breadcrumbs, sprinkle of parm, and additional dressing.
